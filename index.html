How it works

Four steps. One real connection.

No infinite feeds. No burnout. Just a focused path from sign-up to a genuine match.

01

Onboard

Tell us who you are, what you're looking for, and how you like to connect. Takes 4 minutes.

02

Get matched

Our weighted scoring engine pairs you with one person whose intent and interests truly align.

03

A private window opens. Daily icebreakers guide you from light banter to what really matters.

04

Reveal, meet, or just stay connected

After day 7 — reveal together, get a real world meetup suggestion, or add each other to your Circle. The relationship doesn't have to end because the window did.

Verified humans only

Every person here is real

Anonymous to your match. Never anonymous to us. Every Veil user is verified with a government ID and random liveness checks. Your identity stays hidden from your match — but bots, scammers, and fake profiles are eliminated before they ever reach you.
